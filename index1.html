1)NITK RACING AND BAJA NITK(DO CLICK HERE FOR MORE INSIGHTSOF THIS EVENT)

Formula 1. Burning rubber on tarmac. PISTONS, GEARS and CRANKSHAFTS working in tandem to send a 700kg car screaming down the track. If this fascinates you then "NITK RACING And BAJA" are the place for you:

NITK Racing and BAJA are very prestigious clubs that accept students to build cars and race them in the intercollegiate competitions.The Entrance process is highly Competitive and consists of a written test followed by several rounds of interviews.The Selection for those interested in building the electronics of car in the ODD SEM andfor those interested in the mechanical aspects and building the actual car (engine, chassis, suspension, etc) happens in the EVEN SEM.

As mentioned it is *HIGHLY COMPETITIVE* and students study for months about cars to crack the test and interviews. The lucky few who get selected build a car to take part in FSAE and BAJA, two intercollegiate competitions organised by the Society for Automotive Engineers (SAE).

The BAJA Team builds an All Terrain Vehicle (ATV) to take part in the BAJA Competition. Students have to take part in all stages of these competitions, such as marketing and fund raising and not just building the car. Students have to stay back in college for a couple of weeks in the holidays, but the experience and knowledge gained is well worth the sacrifice.

2)LSD(DO CLICK HERE FOR MORE INSIGHTSOF THIS EVENT)

LSD or Literary, Stage and Debating Society primarily has three branches that work in it, the Quizzing Team, Debating Society(popularly known as DebSoc) and Lit Gig and the good news is First years can be a part of all three as well!

Quizzing organizes quizzes, debates, plays and much, much more. Usually these are team events (2-3 people) and are good fun to participate in. Quizzes on movies, tech, G.K., lit and any topic they can think of are conducted regularly in both semesters.

Apart from that they regularly hold Debating Sessions which is conducted by DebSoc. There is also a branch called LitGig which is basically the Writer's Club of NITK. This year onwards LSD has started organizing plays which are produced and directed by LSD itself.

3)E-CELL(DO CLICK HERE FOR MORE INSIGHTSOF THIS EVENT)

"Entrepreneurship IS LIVING A FEW YEARS OF YOUR LIFE LIKE MOST PEOPLE WON'T, SO THAT YOU CAN SPEND THE REST OF YOUR LIFE LIKE MOST PEOPLE CAN'T."

E-CELL is the raison d'etre (as its more verbose members would say) to disseminate entrepreneurship education among students, and encourage them to come up with unique ideas - and then build on those ideas to start their own venture. E-CELL was awarded the best technical school promoting entrepreneurship education in India by Intel and NITIE, in 2012.

E-CELL provides entrepreneurship education, incubation facility to start-ups and conducts flagship B-Plan and other Business events. E-CELL has a robust network of budding entrepreneurs and partner E-CELLS across India, as well as an enthusiastic alumni network. E-CELL is a part of National Entrepreneurship Network (NEN) and partners with NITK-STEP and Department of Science and Technology (DST), Govt. of India.

4)DDFC(DO CLICK HERE FOR MORE INSIGHTSOF THIS EVENT)

Dancing to the latest tunes, setting the ramp on fire or enthralling the audience with your performances - if you thought you wouldn’t get to do all this, you were wrong.

Presenting the Dance, Dramatics and Fashion Club, or fondly known as DDFC; the chance to unleash the performer in you.

They conduct major cultural programmes throughout the year. The first one is Spandan which is an opportunity for the first years to showcase their talents. It involves various dance and drama competitions and the hunt for Mr and Miss Glamouranza. In the ODD SEMESTER DDFC also organises Dandiya night!

The beginning of the EVEN SEMESTER is marked by the momentous Bharat Darshan organised on the occasion of Republic Day. Various states have to present their culture in the allotted time and the best one is adjudged the winner. This is the only event which involves most of the first years.

A great chance for non-performers to also overcome their apprehensions and participate - believe me, events like these make college life memorable! It is also a golden opportunity to interact with seniors. DDFC also organises ‘Jukebox’ which involves Pictionary, Dumb charades and fun movie quizzes. As first years one can participate in all these events but the recruitment is done in second year only. The recruitment process consists of resume writing and then audition (with Personal Interview). You can contact any senior for details regarding the interviews. In NITK , no interview is totally technical. The seniors will always be playful with you. In DDFC, you will be asked to showcase the best of your talents.
